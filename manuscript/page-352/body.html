“I should think Marilla’s raspberry cordial would prob’ly be much nicer than Mrs. Lynde’s,” said Anne loyally. “Marilla is a famous cook. The last time I made a cake I forgot to put the flour in. I was thinking the loveliest story about you and me, Diana.
